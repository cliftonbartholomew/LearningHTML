Bart Bartholomew

Trust him, he is an engineer.

Here's a time line of Bart's life:

  • 1963 - Born in Toti, South Africa
  • 1981 - Attends the University of Kwa-Zulu Natal to study a Bachelors of Mechanical Engineering
  • 1985 - Gets engaged to Judy Kenton
  • 1989 - Marries Judy Kenton
  • 1991 - First son, Clifton, is born in Westville hospital
  • 1993 - Second son, Mervyn, is born in Westville hospital
  • 2001 - Starts his own company from home, Nautilus Engineers, with four employees
  • 2006 - Nautilus Engineers grows to 8 people and moves into their own offices in Umhlali
  • 2007 - Divorces Judy
  • 2010 - Meets Lene, his now current partner at Salties, a reknowned establishment in Salt Rock
  • 2019 - Buys a farm in Mooi River where he moves to with Lene and now currently resides

"I went to the woods because I wished to live deliberately, to front only the essential facts of life, and see if I could learn what it had to teach, and not, when I came to die, discover that I had not lived. I did not wish to live what was not life, living is so dear; nor did I wish to practise resignation, unless it was quite necessary. I wanted to live deep and suck all the marrow out of life, to live so sturdily and Spartan-like as to put to rout all that was not life, to cut a broad swath and shave close, to drive life into a corner, and reduce it to its lowest terms."

-- Henry David Thoreau
